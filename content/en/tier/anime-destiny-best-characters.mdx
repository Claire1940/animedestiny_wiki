<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Traits and Reroll Strategy">

Traits act as passive modifiers that can dramatically alter a character's damage output, attack range, or placement cost. Having the right trait on an S-Tier unit is what separates a standard build from a high-performance meta setup.

<Callout type="info" title="Trait Priority">
The Divine trait is currently the best overall modifier for raw DPS units, offering a massive boost to base attack stats. Stellar is highly recommended for units that rely on damage-over-time effects like burn or bleed.
</Callout>

| Trait Name | Stat Modifiers | Optimal Characters | Reroll Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|

| **Stellar** | High Attack & Speed Buff | Ashura Swordsman (3SS), Insect Poison | **Keep** for multi-hit and damage-over-time characters. |
| **Sniper** | Crit Chance & Range Increase | Eternal Night (Blood), Radiant Hunter | **Keep** on characters built for single-target boss killing. |
| **Tycoon** | Extra Money Generation | Bulm, Cyan Idol | **Keep** on economy/support characters to fund early waves. |

When rolling for traits using Reroll Shards (RR), always prioritize your main damage dealer first. Once your primary carry has a top-tier trait like Divine or Stellar, you can begin optimizing your secondary supports and economy units with traits like Tycoon.
