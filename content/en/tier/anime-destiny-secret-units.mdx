Frozen Moon (Kizuki) is the premier crowd control and shield-breaking unit. His attacks apply a freeze effect that halts fast-moving waves, making him indispensable for high-wave Endless Trials. 

### Founding Titan (Queen)
Founding Titan (Queen) excels at wide-area destruction. Her summon mechanic calls mini-titans onto the battlefield, creating a secondary defensive wall that absorbs damage while dealing massive AoE damage to boss targets.

### Shadow Monarch (Awaken) & Crimson Knight (Dark)
These two units share an incredible elemental synergy. Shadow Monarch (Awaken) summons dark shadows that inherit a portion of his stats, while Crimson Knight (Dark) deals massive slash damage. When deployed together, their dark synergy multiplies overall damage output.

### The Power of the Divine Trait
The **Divine** trait is the undisputed best-in-slot modifier for every Secret carry. It provides a massive flat damage multiplier that scales perfectly with the high base stats of Secret units. If you roll Divine on Kizuki, Queen, or Awaken, lock the trait immediately and do not replace it.

### Artifact Selection Guidelines
Artifacts add another layer of customization. The Galactic Scouter, Strongest's Coat, and Strongest's Tricorn are the most sought-after accessories. They boost range, critical strike chance, and base attack power.
