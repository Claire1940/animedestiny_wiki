<Section icon={Swords} title="S-Tier: Meta DPS and Utility">

Characters in the S-tier represent the peak of performance in the current game version. These units offer exceptional shield breaking, freeze utility, or massive area-of-effect damage. Investing in these characters ensures your squad can handle high-difficulty boss fights and deep endless runs.

Below is the detailed breakdown of the S-tier units, including their optimal setups.

| Unit Name | Rarity | Primary Role | Optimal Trait | Top Artifacts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Frozen Moon (Kizuki) | Secret | Freeze Utility / DPS | Divine | Galactic Scouter / Strongest's Coat / Strongest's Tricorn |
| Founding Titan (Queen) | Secret | Full AOE / Summons | Divine | Galactic Scouter / Strongest's Coat / Strongest's Tricorn |

**Frozen Moon (Kizuki)** provides crucial crowd control. Her freezing abilities stall fast-moving waves, giving your primary damage dealers time to clear the board. Her shield-breaking value makes her indispensable in high-tier content.

**Founding Titan (Queen)** excels at clearing large groups. Her Titan summons draw enemy aggression and deal consistent AOE damage, making her a top choice for both story stages and raids.

**Shadow Monarch (Awaken)** acts as a hybrid force. He utilizes dark synergy, summoning shadows to fight alongside him while maintaining high base attack power.

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Priority">

| Eternal Night (Blood) | Mythic | Boss Killer / Crit DPS | Sniper | Titan Slayer Blades / Scout Cloak |

| Insect Slayer (Poison) | Mythic | Damage-Over-Time | Divine or Stellar | Galactic Scouter / Strongest's Coat / Strongest's Tricorn |
| Magicbane (Anti-Magic) | Mythic | Heavy Shield Breaker | Divine | Galactic Scouter / Strongest's Coat / Strongest's Tricorn |

**Eternal Night (Blood)** scales incredibly well with critical strike builds. In boss encounters, her scaling lets her melt high-health targets faster than standard AOE characters.

**Future Warrior (Furious)** provides sustained single-target damage. He is a great option for lane defense against large minibosses that slip past your main line.

**Insect Slayer (Poison)** applies poison stacks to entire waves. This damage bypasses standard defensive shields, making her a safe pick for stages with high-defense enemies.

<Callout type="info" title="Shield Mechanics">
Magicbane (Anti-Magic) should be placed near the start of your defense line. His ability to strip shields early allows your pure DPS characters to deal maximum damage downstream.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait Synergies & Reroll Strategy">

Traits modify your units' performance significantly. Matching the right trait to a character's core role is essential for optimizing your team's damage output.

Use the steps below to systematically roll and lock the best traits for your squad.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Reroll Resources (RR)">
    Gather Reroll Shards (RR) from daily challenges, weekly milestones, and active codes like "Release!" or "SorryForDelay".
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify High-Tier Targets">
    Select only evolved Mythic or Secret units for trait customization. Never spend RR on temporary legendary units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Target Role-Specific Traits">
    Roll for Divine on raw DPS carries, Sniper on single-target boss killers, and Tycoon on economy support units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lock and Evolve">
    Once a top-tier trait is secured, lock the unit and focus your upgrade materials on maximizing their level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The table below outlines the functionality of each major trait and which units benefit from them the most.

| Trait Name | Type | Best Fit | Recommended Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Divine | Attack / Speed | S-Tier Carries (Kizuki, Queen, Awaken) | Lock immediately |
| Stellar | DoT / Attack | DoT Characters (3SS, Poison) | Keep for DoT units |
| Sniper | Range / Crit | Crit Scale Units (Blood, Light) | Keep for boss killers |
| Tycoon | Economy | Support / Farm Units (Bulm, Cyan Idol) | Keep on money makers |
| Jack of All | Balanced | Legendary Starters (Yellow Flash, Hawkeye) | Replace in late-game |

<Callout type="success" title="Trait Matchup">
Securing a Divine trait on Kizuki or Queen boosts their overall damage output by a massive margin, allowing you to push much further in Endless Trials.
</Callout>
