<Section icon={Trophy} title="Summon Currency & Gems Budgeting">

To consistently pull on the **anime destiny banner**, you must master the game's economy. Gems are the primary currency for summons, while Reroll Shards (RR) are utilized to customize unit traits after they have been summoned. Confusing the two or spending them inefficiently will halt your roster progression.

For new players, redeeming starter codes is the fastest way to build a budget. Using codes like `Release!` gives you an instant 1,500 Gems and 15 RR, while `SorryForDelay` adds another 500 Gems, 5 RR, 5 Cubes, and 5 Malice to your inventory. Beyond codes, repeatable PvE content is your main source of income.

| Mode | Gems Yield | Challenge Frequency | Best Setup |

| Story Stages | Moderate | One-time first clear | Complete with basic Legendary units |
| Igris Boss Event | High | Event-based repeat | Use freeze stall and high single-target DPS |

Avoid the temptation to spend Gems on individual single summons. Multi-summons are more efficient and allow you to leverage the free Auto Roll feature to filter out unwanted Rare and Epic units automatically. Keep a reserve of at least 5,000 Gems before target-pulling a featured Mythic unit.
