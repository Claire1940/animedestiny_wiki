<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Summon Rates and Pity Mechanics">

Summoning is the core progression loop in Anime Destiny. To build a squad capable of clearing high-level Story stages, Boss Challenges, and Endless Trials, you must optimize how you spend Gems. Banners rotate regularly in the lobby, featuring a mix of Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic, and Secret units. Without a structured approach, you risk wasting rare currencies on low-tier pulls.

The pity system exists to safeguard your investment. Every summon that does not yield a high-rarity unit ticks your pity counter upward. Once the counter reaches its threshold, your next summon guarantees a unit of that specific rarity tier. 

| Rarity | Base Summon Rate | Pity Guarantee Threshold | Primary Utility |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rare | High | None | Early game filler, quick progression |
| Epic | Moderate | None | Mid-tier support, early defense |
| Legendary | Low | Guaranteed at 50 pulls | Transition carries, early boss killers |
| Mythic | Very Low | Guaranteed at 250 pulls | Meta DPS, essential team anchors |
| Secret | Extremely Low | Dynamic progression tracking | Absolute game carries, high investment |

<Callout type="warning" title="Pity Reset Rule">
Pulling a Mythic or Legendary unit naturally before hitting the pity threshold resets that specific pity counter back to zero. Keep a close eye on your counter in the summon UI to plan your Gem spending.
</Callout>

Understanding the relationship between base rates and pity limits is crucial for resources planning. The table below outlines how pity counters operate across different banner types.

| Banner Type | Pity Type | Shared Across Banners | Best Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard Banner | Legendary & Mythic | Yes | Build pity here, execute on S-Tier rotations |
| Event Banner | Event Exclusive | No | Pull only if you have enough Gems to hit pity |
| Secret Banner | Secret Tracker | Yes | Long-term accumulation for high-end carries |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Summon & Pity Management">

Executing a successful summoning strategy requires patience and strict resource management. New players often make the mistake of summoning immediately after earning a few hundred Gems. Follow this systematic approach to secure S-tier units without wasting resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Starter Codes First">
    Before pulling on any banner, navigate to the Codes menu on the right side of the screen. Input active codes like `Release!` and `SorryForDelay` to claim free Gems, Reroll Shards (RR), and upgrade materials. This initial pool gives you enough capital to bypass the weakest early-game progression phases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Analyze Current Banner Rotations">
    Check the active banners in the lobby. Do not summon if the featured Mythic or Secret units belong to lower tiers. Compare the featured banner units against S-tier carries such as Frozen Moon (Kizuki) or Founding Titan (Queen). If the current rotation lacks high-impact DPS or utility, save your Gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prepare Your Gem Bank">
    Do not begin a summon session unless you have enough Gems to secure your targeted pity. If you are chasing a Mythic unit, ensure you have the required Gems to hit the 250-pull safety net. Relying on raw luck without a safety net often results in half-completed pity tracks when banners rotate out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Free Auto Roll">
    Anime Destiny features a free Auto Roll mechanic accessible directly from the summon menu. Configure the Auto Roll settings to automatically stop when you pull a Legendary, Mythic, or Secret unit. This saves time and prevents you from accidentally summoning past your target unit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
