<Section icon={Target} title="Progression Guide: From Starter to End-Game">

Progression requires careful management of your summon budget and upgrade materials. New players should focus on clearing the initial story stages to unlock repeatable farming routes. This provides a steady income of Gems and upgrade resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Codes">
    Before entering your first battle, open the Codes panel on the right side of the screen. Enter active codes such as **Release!** and **SorryForDelay** to secure a starting pool of Gems, Trait Rerolls, Cubes, and Malice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble a Balanced Starter Squad">
    Spend your initial Gems on the basic summon banner. Aim for a mix of cheap ground units, an early shield breaker like Crow Prodigy, and a reliable Legendary DPS like Yellow Flash or Hawkeye.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Push Story Progression">
    Complete Story Mode stages sequentially. This unlocks higher-tier rewards, Namak Planet Hard, and access to time-limited events like the Igris Boss Event.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm Upgrade & Evolution Materials">
    Collect Stat Cubes, Malice, and unit-specific evolution items. Prioritize evolving your main Mythic or Secret carries to unlock their full skill trees and secondary abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Optimize with Traits and Artifacts">
    Once your core squad is evolved, spend your saved Trait Rerolls (RR) to secure Divine or Stellar traits. Equip matching artifacts to maximize damage output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Trait Reroll & Resource Strategy">

Traits act as passive multipliers that can completely change a unit's viability. Rolling the correct trait determines whether a unit functions as a top-tier damage carry, a support utility, or an economy generator. Because Trait Rerolls (RR) are limited, resource management is crucial.

| Trait / Resource | Type | Optimal Use Case | Strategic Advice |
|---|---|---|---|
| **RR** | Resource | Rerolling unit traits | Save exclusively for Mythic and Secret units |
| **Divine** | Trait | High-end DPS carries | Keep immediately; provides the highest raw damage scaling |
| **Stellar** | Trait | DoT and shield breakers | Excellent on units like Ashura Swordsman or Insect Slayer |
| **Sniper** | Trait | Crit-scaling single-target DPS | Best on boss killers like Eternal Night and Radiant Hunter |
| **Tycoon** | Trait | Economy and support units | Keep on farm units like Bulm or Cyan Idol to boost money generation |
| **Jack of All** | Trait | Starter Legendary units | Accept on temporary units; do not waste heavy RR chasing perfect rolls |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Allocation Warning">
Do not spend your Trait Rerolls on Rare, Epic, or base Legendary units. These units will eventually be replaced by Mythic and Secret alternatives, making early RR investment a waste of valuable resources.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="PvE Content & End-Game Challenges">

Once your squad is optimized, the end-game consists of three main pillars: high-difficulty Story stages, Boss Challenges, and Endless Trials. Coordinating unit placement and upgrade priority is essential for surviving the late-game waves.

- **Story Stages**: The primary source of early Gems and account experience. Completing these unlocks harder variations and challenges.
- **Boss Challenges**: Single-target DPS checks, such as the Igris Boss Event, which reward exclusive upgrade materials and high-tier artifacts.
- **Endless Trials**: Test your squad's endurance against scaling enemy health and shield values. This is the ultimate test of your placement strategy and trait optimization.
