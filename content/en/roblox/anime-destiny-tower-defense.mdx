<Section icon={Shield} title="PvE Progression Routes and Checklists">

To test your squad's limits, progress through different PvE modes. Each mode serves a distinct purpose in your resource farming cycle.

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Sequencing">
Do not jump straight into Endless Trials with base units. You will get overrun quickly, wasting time that could be spent farming evolution drops in Planet Namak (Hard) or taking down boss challenges.
</Callout>

- **Story Stages**: Your main progression route. Clearing stages grants Gems, unlocks new worlds, and opens up higher tier challenge modes.
- **Boss Challenges**: Single-target fights like the Igris Boss Event. These test your direct DPS and reward high-tier upgrade materials.
- **Endless Trials**: Survival maps designed to test team synergy, placement speed, and buff management. Use these to farm EXP and leaderboard rankings.
- **Raids & Co-op**: Multi-player lobbies that allow you to pool your resources with other players. Ideal for farming exclusive evolution items.
