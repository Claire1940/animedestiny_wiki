<Section icon={Shield} title="Traits and Reroll Guide">

Traits are passive modifiers assigned to units upon summoning. These modifiers can drastically alter a unit's damage output, range, attack speed, or utility. You can reroll these traits using Reroll Resources (RR) at the dedicated NPC in the lobby.

| Trait Name | Type | Best Fit | Action Advice |
|---|---|---|---|
| Divine | Attack / Utility | S-Tier Carries, Secret Units | Keep immediately; do not reroll |
| Stellar | DoT / Shield Break | Ashura Swordsman, Insect Slayer | Keep for damage-over-time units |
| Sniper | Crit / Single-Target | Eternal Night, Radiant Hunter | Keep for boss killers |
| Tycoon | Economy / Support | Bulm, Cyan Idol | Keep on money-generating units |
| Jack of All | Balanced Modifier | Legendary Starter Units | Accept for early game; do not chase |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not waste Reroll Resources (RR) on Rare, Epic, or temporary Legendary units. Save your bulk RR for Mythic and Secret units that will remain in your primary squad permanently.
</Callout>

Matching the correct trait to a unit's role is more important than chasing a single label. For instance, putting a Tycoon trait on a pure damage dealer like Frozen Moon reduces your overall squad DPS. Always align your trait rerolls with the unit's primary function in battle.

</Section>
