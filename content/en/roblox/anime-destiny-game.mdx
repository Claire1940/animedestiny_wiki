Traits act as permanent passive multipliers for your units. Utilizing your Reroll Shards (RR) efficiently is crucial, as wasting them on temporary units will slow your progression when transitioning to late-game maps.

| Trait / Resource | Type | Primary Function | Ideal Targets | Action Advice |

To construct a high-performance roster, you must balance summon spending with material farming. Spending all your Gems on the standard banner without checking current banners is a common beginner mistake.

### Economy and Progression Loop

| Progression Area | Primary Cost | Source Location | Focus Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| Summoning | Gems (50 per pull) | Story stages, daily challenges | High (Early game) |
| Unit Upgrading | Gold / Cubes | Story clear rewards, milestones | High (Mid game) |

### Summoning Strategy
1. **Focus on Banners**: Check the active banner in the lobby before spending Gems. Summon only when a high-tier Mythic or Secret unit has an increased drop rate.
2. **Utilize Auto Roll**: Set up the free Auto Roll filter to stop automatically when a Legendary, Mythic, or Secret character is pulled.
3. **Upgrade Priorities**: Once deployed, focus your in-battle gold upgrades on your primary AoE damage dealer first. Do not distribute upgrades evenly across all units.

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Tip">
If you hit a wall in the story mode, return to previous stages on Hard difficulty. This allows you to accumulate evolution materials and Gems without wasting energy on failed attempts.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="PvE Game Modes & Tactical Walkthrough">

The game's PvE structure progresses from testing strategies in Story Stages to optimizing team composition for Boss Challenges and testing endurance in Endless Trials.

### PvE Progression Steps
- **Story Stages**: Your primary source of experience and starter Gems. Focus on clearing these to unlock harder gameplay elements.
- **Boss Challenges**: Harder fights (such as the Igris Boss Event) that test your single-target DPS and shield-breaking capability.
- **Endless Trials**: Survival maps designed to test unit scaling. Strategic placement of freeze units is critical to slowing fast-moving targets.
- **Solo vs Co-Op**: Play solo to learn wave timings, and group up for Endless Trials or Hard Boss raids to share the defense load.
