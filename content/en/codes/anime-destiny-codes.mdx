<Section icon={Target} title="PvE Modes & Farming Routes">

Progression in the game is split across several distinct PvE modes. Each mode serves a specific purpose in your upgrading loop. Pushing into harder content too quickly will result in defeats if your squad is under-leveled or lacks proper traits.

- **Story Stages**: The baseline mode for testing your squad. Clearing these stages unlocks new maps, increases your level cap, and provides a steady source of Gems.
- **Boss Challenges**: High-intensity fights against single targets with massive health pools. These challenges test your single-target DPS and require units like Founding Titan or Eternal Night.
- **Endless Trials**: A survival mode where waves scale in difficulty indefinitely. This is the premier mode for testing late-game squad composition, placement efficiency, and sustain.

<Callout type="warning" title="Endless Trials Scaling">
Endless Trials feature scaling shields and immunities on enemy waves. Ensure your squad contains at least one shield breaker, such as Frozen Moon (Kizuki) or Ashura Swordsman (3SS), before attempting deep runs.
</Callout>

To optimize your farming time, focus on repeatable challenges. Daily and weekly quests offer guaranteed Reroll Shards, while high-difficulty maps like Namak Planet (Hard) yield the Stat Cubes required to maximize your units' base parameters.
