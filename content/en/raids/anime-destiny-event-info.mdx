<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Traits & Reroll Strategy">

Traits are passive modifiers that can drastically alter a unit's effectiveness. Players can reroll these traits using Reroll Resources (RR). Because RR is a limited resource, players must prioritize which units receive rerolls. Chasing perfect rolls on temporary Legendary or Epic units is a common pitfall that leaves players resource-starved when they finally acquire Secret or Mythic carries.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Avoid spending RR on units below Mythic rarity unless you are struggling to clear basic story content.
</Callout>
