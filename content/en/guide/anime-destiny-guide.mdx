<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Traits and Reroll Mechanics">

Traits are passive modifiers assigned to units upon summoning. These modifiers can drastically alter a unit's range, damage, attack speed, or utility. Players can change these traits using Reroll Shards (RR), which are obtained from codes, daily missions, and challenges.

Understanding when to save and when to spend your Reroll Shards is critical for long-term progression.

| Trait / Resource | Type | Ideal Match | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Reroll Shards (RR)** | Resource | Mythic and Secret units | Save for high-tier DPS carries |
| **Divine** | Trait | Top-tier DPS & Secrets | Keep immediately; do not roll over |
| **Stellar** | Trait | DoT & Shield Breakers | Keep on multi-hit or DoT units |
| **Sniper** | Trait | Crit-scaling & Single-target DPS | Keep on boss-killing units |
| **Tycoon** | Trait | Economy & Support units | Keep on units like Bulm or Cyan Idol |
| **Jack of All** | Trait | Legendary starter units | Accept for early game; do not chase |
| **Cubes** | Resource | Progression system | Farm via repeatable challenges |
| **Malice** | Resource | Progression system | Collect from event codes and raids |

<Callout type="info" title="Economy Units">
Do not roll purely offensive traits like Divine on support units. Economy units like **Bulm** or **Cyan Idol** benefit far more from utility traits like **Tycoon**, which enhance cash generation during matches.
</Callout>

</Section>
