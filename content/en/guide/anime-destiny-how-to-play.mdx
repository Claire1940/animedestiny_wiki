<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Gameplay Progression">

Navigating your first few hours in the game can determine how quickly you transition into late-game raids and endless challenges. Follow this structured roadmap to optimize your progression path.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Launch and Group Join">
    Start by launching the game from the official [Roblox Play Page](https://www.roblox.com/games/82674355647259/Anime-Destiny). Make sure to join the official [Anime Destiny Roblox Group](https://www.roblox.com/communities/859372599/Anime-Destiny-AD) to unlock group-related rewards and ensure you have access to community perks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Claim Code Rewards">
    Navigate to the right side of the screen and click the Codes button. Input the active codes exactly as written to receive your starter Gems, Reroll Shards (RR), Stat Cubes, and Malice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Summon Your First Squad">
    Head to the summon area in the lobby. Use your accumulated Gems to roll for units. Do not worry about getting a perfect Secret unit immediately; focus on securing a balanced team of Rare, Epic, and Legendary units to clear early stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Push Story Stages">
    Enter the Story Mode portals. Focus entirely on clearing stages to unlock new maps, higher difficulty levels, and better farming routes. Story mode is the primary source of early-game experience and resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Upgrade Units In-Battle">
    During active waves, place your units at key choke points. Prioritize upgrading your primary damage dealers first to handle larger waves, then upgrade utility or slowing units to manage boss targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Save Reroll Shards">
    As you accumulate Reroll Shards (RR), resist the urge to spend them on temporary Legendary or Rare units. Save the bulk of your RR for Mythic and Secret units that will remain in your squad long-term.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Farm Repeatable Challenges">
    Once story progression slows down, start farming daily and weekly challenges, 15-minute and 30-minute challenges, and special boss events like the Igris Boss Event to collect upgrade materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Placement Strategy">
Place your high-damage single-target units near the start of the track to break enemy shields early. Place area-of-effect (AOE) and damage-over-time (DoT) units further down the line to clean up groups of weakened enemies.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Traits & Resource Rerolling">

Traits are passive modifiers assigned to units upon summoning. They can drastically alter a unit's range, damage, attack speed, or utility. Utilizing Reroll Shards (RR) allows you to replace undesirable traits with meta-defining options.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Damage Carries">
    For units like **Red Emperor** or **Founding Titan**, aim for the **Divine** or **Stellar** traits to maximize raw damage and damage-over-time output.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crit & Range Units">
    For single-target specialists like **Eternal Night**, the **Sniper** trait provides critical hit scaling and extra range to snipe bosses from afar.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Economy & Support">
    For support units like **Bulm** or **Cyan Idol**, keep the **Tycoon** trait to boost money generation during active waves.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Beginner Fillers">
    For early-game Legendary units, the **Jack of All** trait provides a balanced stat boost without requiring heavy investment.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info" title="Trait Alignment">
Always align a unit's trait with its primary function. Forcing a pure damage trait onto an economy unit or a range trait onto a low-range support unit wastes precious Reroll Shards.
</Callout>

| Resource | Type | Primary Source | Best Spending Strategy |
