<Section icon={Target} title="Trait Rerolling and Economy Management">

Traits add randomized stat modifiers to your units, which can completely alter their performance. Rerolling these traits requires Reroll Shards (RR), which are scarce. Managing your RR economy determines how quickly you can optimize your team for late-game content.

Forcing top-tier traits on temporary Legendary units is a waste of resources. Accept average traits early on and save your bulk RR for Mythics and Secrets.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Rarity">
    Only spend high amounts of RR on units you intend to keep long term, such as evolved Mythics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Match Roles">
    Ensure the rolled trait matches the unit's role. Do not force damage traits on economy support units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Target Divine">
    For main DPS units, roll until you secure Divine or Stellar to maximize raw damage scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lock and Save">
    Once an optimal trait is secured, lock the unit to prevent accidental rerolls and save remaining RR.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The table below highlights the primary traits, their utility, and which units benefit from them the most.

| Trait | Modifier Focus | Target Unit Type | Reroll Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Divine | High DPS increase | Main carries, Secret units | Lock immediately; do not replace |
| Stellar | DoT, Shield Break | Attack speed, DoT units | Keep for units like Ashura Swordsman |
| Sniper | Critical hit scaling | Single-target boss killers | Keep for crit-focused physical builds |
| Tycoon | Money generation | Economy, Support units | Keep on Bulm or Cyan Idol |
| Jack of All | General minor buff | Temporary Legendary units | Accept early; do not waste RR chasing |

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Trap">
Avoid the temptation to chase a perfect Divine trait on base Legendary units. The performance increase is minor compared to simply evolving a Mythic unit.
</Callout>
