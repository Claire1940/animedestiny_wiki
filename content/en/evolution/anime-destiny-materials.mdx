<Section icon={Target} title="Evolution and Upgrade Workflow">

Evolving units is the core mechanism for increasing your squad's power. Follow this workflow to ensure you aren't wasting materials on units that will be replaced.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Unit Utility">
    Check your unit's role against the current meta. Only evolve units that fill a gap in your DPS, shield-breaking, or utility needs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Material Recipes">
    Consult the in-game unit panel or the official Trello to see the exact material requirements for the next evolution tier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Targeted Stages">
    Focus your stamina on stages that drop the specific cubes or malice required for your target unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Evolution">
    Once all materials are gathered, proceed to the evolution station in the lobby to upgrade your unit's stats and ability caps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Waste">
Avoid over-investing in units just because they look cool. A low-tier unit with maxed-out materials will still underperform compared to a base-level Secret unit in endgame content.
</Callout>
