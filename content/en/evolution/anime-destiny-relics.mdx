<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Anime Destiny Relics System and Core Mechanics">

Optimizing your squad in the Roblox tower defense experience requires a deep understanding of **anime destiny relics** (frequently referred to in-game as artifacts). These equippable items provide passive stat boosts that can turn a struggling unit into a map-clearing powerhouse. Unlike basic level-ups, relics modify core attributes such as attack range, flat damage, and critical hit multipliers, making them essential for late-game progression.

Each unit has designated slots for relics, which unlock as you progress and evolve your characters. Equipping the correct relic style ensures your placement cost translates to efficient damage output during intense waves.

<Callout type="info" title="Relic Slots and Equipping">

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Farm and Upgrade Relics">

Acquiring high-tier **anime destiny relics** requires consistent farming across various PvE modes. While basic relics drop from early Story stages, the most powerful artifacts are locked behind Boss Challenges, Endless Trials, and specific time-limited events. 

Once obtained, relics must be leveled up using upgrade materials like Stat Cubes and Malice, which are obtained through daily challenges and redeemable codes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Push Story Mode Stages">
    Clear the main campaign stages to unlock the basic relic drop pool and gather starter equipment for your squad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Boss Challenges">
    Defeat bosses like Igris to obtain rare crafting components, legendary relic drops, and upgrade materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evolve Units to Open Slots">
    Use evolution materials to transform your units, which automatically unlocks their second relic slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Relics with Cubes">
    Spend your accumulated Stat Cubes and Malice in the inventory menu to level up your equipped relics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not waste high-grade upgrade materials on temporary Rare or Epic relics. Save your Stat Cubes for Mythic and Legendary artifacts that will remain in your active squad long-term.
</Callout>

| Game Mode | Primary Relic Drop | Upgrade Resource | Difficulty Rating |
|---|---|---|---|
| Story Stages | Common to Rare Relics | Gold / Starter XP | Easy to Medium |
| Boss Challenges | Legendary Relics | Stat Cubes / Malice | Hard |
| Endless Trials | Relic Crafting Materials | Gems / Reroll Shards | Very Hard |
| Namak Planet (Hard) | High-tier Artifacts | Evolution Materials | Hard |
